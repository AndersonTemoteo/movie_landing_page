History

The story of "interstellar" began in 2006, when film producer Linda Obst and famous astrophysicist Kipp Thorn caught fire with the idea to make a picture of intergalactic travel. This is not surprising, because in the scientific world Thorn is known as the author of the theory of "wormholes" - a kind of "holes" in time and space, which connect the remote points of the universe and make possible intergalactic travel. At least in theory.

The action of the film "Interstellar" takes place in the near future. The climate on Earth has changed significantly, and the high concentrantion of nitrogen will soon lead to the death of people. It is urgent to search for another planet in order to move the entire human race to it. Scientists have found a wormhole in space through which it is possible to move to another galaxy. It's the only way to save all humanity...

Plot

In 2067, a global famine caused by ecocide threatens humanity with extinction. Former NASA pilot Joseph Cooper, now a farmer with his family, discovers a gravitational anomaly that leads him to a secret NASA facility. There, he is enlisted to pilot a mission through a wormhole near Saturn to find habitable planets. Cooper joins a team, including Amelia Brand, and they travel through the wormhole, facing dangerous conditions on potential new worlds.

On an ocean planet, a tidal wave kills one crew member, and due to time dilation, 23 years pass on Earth and their spacecraft. On Earth in 2092, Cooper's daughter Murph works with NASA scientist Brand on a gravity equation to save humanity, which she learns was a false hope intended to prevent panic. Determined, she continues the research and returns home for clues.

On a second planet, Cooper and the team face betrayal and death from Dr. Mann, who falsified data. Surviving an explosive trap and a failed docking attempt, Cooper and Amelia are forced to approach a black hole. Cooper detaches from the main craft to allow Amelia to reach the third planet. Inside the black hole, Cooper communicates with Murph through a tesseract, enabling her to solve the gravity equation.

Rescued near Saturn in 2156, Cooper reunites with the elderly Murph, who urges him to find Amelia. He sets off with TARS to join Amelia, who is establishing a new colony on a habitable planet.
